# Clearance Rules

This skill uses conservative mains-related spacing thresholds for fast screening.
Use design standards and your certification target as final authority.

## Distance Units
- Internal computation: mil
- Display: mil and mm
- Conversion: 1 mil = 0.0254 mm

## Mains-to-Low-Voltage Edge Clearance Screening
- High risk: < 236 mil (6.0 mm)
- Medium risk: 236 to 315 mil (6.0 to 8.0 mm)
- Low risk: >= 315 mil (8.0 mm)

## Notes
- Result is a geometric estimate from pin-pad coordinates.
- Route-level creepage can be worse than pad-edge spacing.
- If board has slots/cutouts, include them in final creepage review.
